My Favorite Book: "The Great gatsby"

"The Great gatsby" is a novel written by F.Scott Fitzgerald and was first published in 1925. it is widely considered one of the greatest American novel. The story is set during the Roaring Twenties and explores themes of love, wealth, and the American Dream.


The novel's main character, Jay Gatsby, is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan. His story ultimately leads to tragic consequences. the book is known for its symbolism and its criticle view of American Dream.
